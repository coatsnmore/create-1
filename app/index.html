Dizzle Dazzle

How to Play

  1. Shoot bad guys for points.
  2. Don't fly into them.
  3. Don't go out of bounds.

Controls

  1. Up = Up
  2. Down = Down
  3. Left = Left
  4. Right = Right
  5. Space = Fire

Caveat

  1. This is a development build.
  2. Performance may vary by computer.
  3. The site currently requires a keyboard and mouse.
